Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Galaxy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our proxy services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and any messages you send to us through forms, feedback submissions, or communication channels.

2. Ezoic Services

This website uses services provided by Ezoic Inc. (“Ezoic”) to manage third-party interest-based advertising. Ezoic may use various technologies, including first-party and third-party cookies, tags, pixels, and beacons, to deliver and optimize advertisements and monitor interactions.

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device to help websites remember information about your browsing session. First-party cookies are created by the site you visit; third-party cookies are set by other domains. Disabling cookies may limit access to parts of the website. Rejected cookies may still result in non-personalized advertisements.

The following information may be collected, stored, and used by Ezoic when serving personalized ads:

  • IP address
  • Operating system type and version
  • Device type
  • Language preferences
  • Web browser type
  • Email (hashed or encrypted)

Ezoic and its partners may combine this information with other independently collected data, such as device identifiers, advertising IDs, geolocation data, referral sources, usage data, and user interactions, to create audience segments for targeted advertising across multiple devices, browsers, and platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services
  • Personalize user experience
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Manage advertising and deliver relevant advertisements
  • Analyze website usage and trends

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing them with your personal information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Changes to the policy are effective when posted on this page.
